Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Qsisto, operated by AB Empire Oy, uses cookies and similar technologies on our websites and products, and how you can control them.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow a site to recognize your device and remember certain information, such as your preferences or login state.

2. Our Approach to Consent

We do not load non-essential cookies or tracking scripts until you have made a choice in our cookie banner. You can choose to:

  • Accept all — allow essential and non-essential cookies
  • Reject non-essential — allow only strictly necessary cookies required for the site to function
  • Manage preferences — choose which categories of non-essential cookies to allow

Your choice is recorded with a timestamp so we can demonstrate compliance with applicable consent requirements. You can change your choice at any time using the button below.

3. Categories of Cookies We Use

CategoryPurposeRequires consent?
Strictly necessarySession handling, security, remembering your cookie choiceNo — always active
FunctionalRemembering preferences such as language or display settingsYes
AnalyticsUnderstanding aggregate usage patterns to improve the productYes

We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Where you complete a payment, Stripe may set cookies necessary for fraud prevention and secure checkout. These are considered strictly necessary for the payment to function and are not subject to the same consent banner, as they only activate during an active checkout you initiate.

5. How Long We Keep Consent Records

Your cookie consent choice is stored for 12 months, after which you will be asked again.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
