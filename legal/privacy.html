3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account and subscription
  • To process payments via Stripe and maintain billing records
  • To deliver the product or service you have subscribed to (for example, monitoring alerts, automated reports, or data analysis)
  • To send essential service communications, including onboarding emails and account notices
  • To respond to support requests sent to support@qsisto.com
  • To comply with legal and tax obligations under Finnish and EU law

We do not use your personal data for non-essential tracking, advertising, or profiling unless you have explicitly given consent via our cookie banner.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to provide the subscribed product and handle billing
  • Legal obligation — accounting and tax record-keeping required under Finnish law
  • Consent — non-essential cookies and any optional marketing communications
  • Legitimate interest — fraud prevention, service security, and responding to support requests

6. Data Retention

We retain account and billing data for as long as your subscription is active and for up to 7 years afterward to comply with Finnish accounting law. Support communications are retained for 2 years. Cookie consent records are retained for 12 months from the date of consent.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to our legal retention obligations
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a portable copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi)

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@qsisto.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), access-controlled databases, and credential-based authentication for all internal systems. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Transfers

Some of our processors (including Stripe and Anthropic) may process data outside the EEA. Where this occurs, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards as required under GDPR.

10. Children's Privacy

Our products are intended for business use by individuals 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of our products after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
