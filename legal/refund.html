Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This policy explains how to cancel your subscription and when a refund may be available for any product operated under the Qsisto portfolio by AB Empire Oy.

1. Cancelling Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future billing — it does not automatically refund payments already made.

How to cancel

Email support@qsisto.com with the subject line "Cancellation Request" and the email address used for your subscription. We aim to confirm cancellation within 2 business days.

Request Cancellation

Once cancelled, you retain access to the product until the end of your current billing period. No further charges will be made.

2. Refund Eligibility

We review refund requests individually rather than offering automatic refunds. As a general guide:

  • Within 14 days of first payment: If you have not received meaningful value from the product (for example, the monitoring, report, or tool never activated due to a fault on our end), you are eligible for a full refund.
  • After 14 days, or for renewal payments: Refunds are considered case by case, for example in the event of a verified service outage, billing error, or duplicate charge.
  • Change of mind after the product has been actively delivering value is generally not eligible for a refund, but you can still cancel future billing at any time.

We do not promise an automatic or guaranteed refund for every request. Each request is reviewed by a person before a decision is made.

3. How to Request a Refund

How to request a refund

Email support@qsisto.com with the subject line "Refund Request," the email address used for your subscription, and a short explanation of the reason for your request.

Request a Refund

4. Processing Time

Refund and cancellation requests go through the following stages:

  • Received — your request has been logged and you will receive a confirmation
  • Processing — we are reviewing the request and, if approved, issuing the refund via Stripe
  • Resolved — the request is complete; for approved refunds, funds are returned to your original payment method, typically within 5–10 business days depending on your bank

5. Disputes and Chargebacks

If you believe a charge was made in error, please contact us first at support@qsisto.com before initiating a chargeback with your bank. We aim to resolve billing issues directly and quickly.
