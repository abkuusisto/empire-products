What we check

Every scan covers these signals

Homepage reachability

Is your store loading? We check HTTP 200 and fast first response.

HTTPS & SSL validity

Expired SSL silently blocks checkout in modern browsers.

Shopify detection

Confirms your store is running Shopify and core assets load.

Cart path accessible

We verify /cart returns a valid response as a proxy for cart functionality.

Checkout signals

Checks for Shopify checkout script presence and cart action signals.

Trust signals

Refund policy, shipping info, and trust badge presence — key conversion elements.

Response time

Slow stores lose mobile shoppers. We track load speed and flag regressions.

Week-over-week diff

Every check is compared to last week. We surface regressions, not just failures.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to install a Shopify app?

No. StoreGuard works entirely from the outside — a read-only scan against your public store URL, exactly like a customer browsing. Nothing is installed in your Shopify admin.

Do you access my Shopify admin?

Never. We have no access to your backend, orders, customers, or settings. We read your public-facing HTML only, the same as any browser would.

Do you complete test purchases?

No. We don’t add items to cart, submit payment forms, or initiate any transaction. Our scan is entirely read-only.

What happens if an issue is found?

You receive an email alert immediately with the specific check that changed and what to investigate first. You take action — we don’t make changes to your store.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No annual commitments or lock-in. Cancel from your account or email support@qsisto.com — confirmed within one business day.

Is this a replacement for full QA testing?

No. StoreGuard is an early-warning monitoring layer, not a QA substitute. Think of it as your store’s smoke alarm — not a full fire suppression system.
