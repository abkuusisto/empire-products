What we check

Every scan covers these signals

Homepage reachability

HTTP 200 and fast first response.

HTTPS & SSL validity

Expired SSL silently blocks checkout in modern browsers.

Shopify detection

Confirms your store is running Shopify and core assets load.

Cart path

Checkout signals

Trust signals

Refund policy, shipping info, and trust badge presence.

Response time

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to install a Shopify app?

No. StoreGuard works entirely from the outside — a read-only scan against your public store URL, exactly like a customer browsing.

Do you access my Shopify admin?

Never. We read your public-facing HTML only, the same as any browser would. No backend, no orders, no customer data.

Do you complete test purchases?

No. We don’t add items to cart, submit payment forms, or initiate any transaction. Our scan is entirely read-only.

What happens if an issue is found?

You receive an email alert immediately with the specific check that changed. You take action — we don’t make changes to your store.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No annual commitments. Cancel from your account or email support@qsisto.com — confirmed within one business day.

Is this a replacement for QA testing?

No. StoreGuard is an early-warning monitoring layer, not a QA substitute. Think of it as your store’s smoke alarm.
